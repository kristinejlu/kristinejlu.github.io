Kristine is a PhD Candidate in Learning Sciences at Northwestern University and a graduate researcher in the interdisciplinary Delta Lab. Her research interests are at the intersection of learning and information environments, particularly data, information, and systems design for civic decision-making. Prior to Northwestern, Kristine conducted journalism research at the Pew Research Center in Washington, D.C., where she worked on a variety of quantitative social science projects related to civic engagement and news attitudes and consumption. Her research experience also includes work in computational linguistics, digital humanities, educational technology, and information literacy. She graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Arts in English & Comparative Literature from Columbia University.
